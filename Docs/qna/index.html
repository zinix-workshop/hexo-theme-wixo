WixoQuestions and Answers 2014-03-16

Q What does Wixo stands for?

A Wixo means A wiki theme for hexo.

Q How to generate ToC(Table of Contents) in a certain page?

A Add toc: true in the front-matter.

Q Where can I find your markdown source files of these docs?

A In the source branch.

Q Why some of my notebooks cannot be opened?

A Do not use spaces when naming your notebooks/categories like “Code Book”. Use _ if you have to e.g. “Code_Book”.
